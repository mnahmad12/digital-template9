ER

  • High Concept: You are playing as an ER nurse in charge of deciding who needs to get treated right away, and who has to wait.
  • Player Experience Goals: To see the pressure the nurse has in the ER, the wrong choice could lead to death!
  • Genre: Puzzle
  • Game play: Patients: rectangles, come into the ER at various stages. Red patients: Most urgent, will die in 15 seconds, need 30 seconds on a bed to recover. Orange: will die in 20 seconds, need 20 to recover on a bed, Yellow: Will die in 30 sec, need 15 to recover, Green: will die in 40 sec, need 10 to recover. Place the patients into the beds in the correct order, and cause the fewest casualties possible!
  • Prototype goal: 1) Timer
  • Features: timers for different players, spontaneous game-play
  • Setting/Premise: You are an ER nurse, and patients are pouring into the hospital. Don't let them die!
